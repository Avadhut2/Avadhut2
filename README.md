# About Me:
Hi, I’m Avadhut Satpute, a first-year Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning engineering student with a strong interest in building intelligent systems that interact with the real world.<br><br>I enjoy turning ideas into practical projects by combining AI, Python, and IoT. My work often focuses on creating systems that are both technically interesting and useful in everyday life. Some of the projects I’m exploring include AI assistants, driver drowsiness detection systems, emotion-based music recommendation (MoodTunes), and decentralized identity verification using blockchain.<br><br>Beyond academics, I’m deeply curious about how intelligent systems work, especially at the intersection of AI, human behavior, and real-world applications. I like experimenting with new tools, learning by building projects, and continuously improving my understanding of technology.<br><br>Outside of development, I’m also passionate about story-driven games and storytelling, which inspires my interest in creating technology that feels more human and meaningful.<br><br>I’m always looking to learn, collaborate, and build projects that push my skills further.
